About Me

I make software. I'm mostly interested in web development as a full-stack developer. I know Pyhton, Javascript, HTML and CSS pretty well. I'm also learning others.

I write longer things on Medium and shorter ones on Twitter. You can check my code on GitHub, verify me on Keybase and tip me on Gratipay.

My latest project is Dropbox Window, which lets you upload files on an untrusted computer by authorizing it using your phone, simply scanning a QR code.

My development environment consists of a Vaio Laptop, Ubuntu, vim, git and tons of free software very smart people selflessly made. I'm grateful to them.

I'm somewhat a free-software and free-speech advocate, I care about equality, diversity and human rights. My favorite tech news site is Ars Technica.
